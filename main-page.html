Card Buster

Card Buster is a merge between action-adventure and deckbuilder genres. In Cardbuster, use Cards to gain abilities. Casting a card fills the matching ability slot to use for a period of time to either defeat enemies. Like classic deckbuilder games you mulligan/draw your hand before a combat begins.

My contributions

  • All development/programming of the game in Unity3D
  • Card ability system, including specific combos and synergies
  • Finite state machines for both player logic and enemy AI
  • Progression and upgrade systems for Player and the Card system
  • VFX implementation
  • Demo release and Steamworks experience

Chasing After the Light

Chasing After the Light is 2022 Global Game Jam (theme: Duality) entry game for our team. Chasing After the Light is a 2D platformer game in which Player tries to stay in the light radius while the light object moves forward on its own (video 1) to keep the sanity level high. Player loses sanity if they stay out of the light (video 2). If the sanity bar drops to zero or player hits the spikes the game ends (video 3). Player gains points as long as they stay alive. Player increases their score multiplier proportional to how close they are to the light source.

My contributions/lessons learned

  • All the programming and level design using Unity3D.
  • Rail system for the movement of light object
  • Scoring system by checking variables like the Player distance from the light, is there any obstacles between the Player and the light
  • Learned the experimental 2D light feature of Unity3D's Universal Render Pipeline to achieve the desired light/dark effect

Back to Hometown

Back to Hometown is a game made for an Itch.io minijam (theme: Colors). In Back to Hometown, Player plays as a wizard who returns to their town after many years to find it corrupted by an alien force. Using Player's abilities, kill monsters and cleanse the area. 1st ability is a single projectile fired from Player's staff (video 1). 2nd ability is an area of effect damage cast in front of the Player (video 1). 3rd ability is an area of effect ability cast around the player which heals themselves and clears the corruption around (video 2). Boss corrupts an adjacent tile every few seconds and corrupted tiles allow portals to open above them that spawn more monsters (video 3).

My contributions

  • All the programming and level design using Unity3D.
  • Tilemap system for the map and the corruption effect
  • Corruption affects both the visuals and the gameplay mechanics
  • Monster AI

Need More Space

Need More Space is Ludum Dare 47 (theme: Stuck in a loop) entry game for our team. In Need More Space, Player needs to find energy crystals in order to power Player's spaceship up to escape the sphere Player is on. Planets are too hot under the sun and too cold at the opposite side. Player needs to bring ice cubes under sun to melt them and drink, lure monsters to the sun to cook them (video 1) then eat, and be aware of Player's own body temperature so that Player doesn't overheat or freeze themselves.

My contributions

    I have programmed monster AI so that they don't wander under the sun themselves and chase after the Player when They get in range (video 2). Since the planet Player is on is stationary, Monster AI checks the "Vector3 nextPoint" 3d position vector they will move next to see if the horizontal x of the nextPoint falls in positive values, if it does then the function determines the nextPoint again randomly. I also implemented the lighting settings and movement of the sun and moon around the planet (video 3).

Life Socks

Life Socks is 2021 Global Game Jam (theme: Lost & Found) entry game for our team. Life Socks is a 2D-platformer-puzzle game in which Player controls a sock who has lost their pair trying to find them. Player has 2 item slots that they can carry either keys to open doors or power-ups (video 1). There are power-ups such as 'Feet' which enable Player to walk faster/jump and if Player has 2 'Feet' items They can double-jump (video 2), 'Umbrella' item enables Player to glide long distances (video 3).

My contributions

    I took part in programming and level design of this game. I also did the implementation of the level in Unity3D.

(Un)reliable Artifact

(Un)reliable Artifact is Ludum Dare 49 (theme: Unstable) entry for our team. In this game Player has an unstable artifact which explodes in random intervals cleansing the corrupted buildings and road tiles in range (video 1). With every cleansed building/tile Player receives points. While cleansing a building restores it, cleansing a road tile destroys the tile (video 2), so Player needs to be careful to not fall into the abyss through destroyed parts of the road (video 3).

My contributions

    I did all the programming and level design while taking part in the game design process. Corruption effect is achieved in a similar manner to "Back to Hometown". The difference is all the buildings and road pieces are stored in "Roads" and "Buildings" lists at the start of the game and removed from it when it gets corrupted.
