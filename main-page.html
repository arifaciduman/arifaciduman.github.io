Card Buster

Card Buster is an action roguelike game with deckbuilding features, set in a vibrant post-apocalyptic world. Use your deck of cards to acitvate powerful abilities in action, become more powerful, discover new cards and upgrade them, and beat the powerful mage Psychic!

Access Card Buster on Steam: https://store.steampowered.com/app/1915400/Card_Buster/

Work done/Challenges

  • All development/programming of the game in Unity3D using C#
  • Card ability system, including specific combos and synergies
  • Finite state machines for both player logic and enemy AI
  • Progression and upgrade systems for Player and the Card system
  • VFX implementation
  • Demo release and Steamworks experience

Combat

  • Top-down action combat system with enemy AI
  • Seperate card types using strong polymorphism and OOP base
  • Card creation system that enables a very easy way to add new cards
  • Extensive card combo system

Deckbuilding

  • Deckbuilding system with intricate mechanics
  • Card Upgrade system, affecting different card types in different ways
  • Card reward system when rooms are completed in game
  • Crafting new cards with recipe system
  • Card data held by Scriptable Objects

Rewards

  • Both in-game rewards that powers the player up as a run continues
  • Meta progression system permanently powering up the player and their cards

Chasing After the Light

Chasing After the Light is 2022 Global Game Jam (theme: Duality) entry game for our team. The Player has to stay in the light radius while the light object moves forward on its own to keep the sanity level high enough.
Genre: Precision platformer
Platform: PC│Web
Tools: Unity3D, Adobe Photoshop
Project Length: 48 hours

Work done/Challenges

  • All the programming and level design using Unity3D.
  • Rail system for the movement of light object.
  • Scoring system by the Player distance from the light, obstacles between the Player and the light, and time alive.
  • Used experimental 2D light feature of Unity3D's Universal Render Pipeline to achieve the desired light/dark effect

Back to Hometown

Back to Hometown is a game made for an Itch.io minijam (theme: Colors). Player has 3 abilities and a boss fight at the end of the game. Enemy boss spreads corruption around the map, and the Player uses their "Replenish" ability to cleanse the corruption and heal. Corrupted tiles enable portals that spawn enemy monsters.

Genre: ARPG
Platform: PC│Web
Tools: Unity3D, Adobe Photoshop
Project Length: 48 hours

Work done/Challenges

  • All the programming and level design using Unity3D.
  • Tilemap system for the map and the Corruption effect.
  • Corruption affects both the visuals and the gameplay mechanics. I had to check the tiles which the portal positions translated to for corruption, and enable spawn mechanic accordingly.

Need More Space

Need More Space is Ludum Dare 47 (theme: Stuck in a loop) entry game for our team. The Player searches energy crystals to fuel their spaceship while keeping their hunger, hydration, and heat meters in check. Game is played on tiny planets, and the "Sun" provides heat at the sun facing side of the planet. Heat is used to kill monsters and cook food, melt ice to make water, and to keep the Player warm.
Genre: Puzzle/Adventure
Platform: PC│Web
Tools: Unity3D, Blender, Adobe Photoshop
Project Length: 72 hours

Work done/Challenges

  • Programmed the monster AI on a tiny spherical planet with custom gravity.
  • Implemented the heat system and the Sun's heat effect on the Player and the environment.
  • Movement of the Sun and the Moon around the planet.

(Un)reliable Artifact

(Un)reliable Artifact is Ludum Dare 49 (theme: Unstable) entry for our team. An unstable artifact affixed to the Player's back explodes in random intervals cleansing the corrupted buildings and road tiles. With every cleansed building/road, the Player receives points. While cleansing a building restores it, cleansing a road tile destroys it, so Player needs to be careful to not fall into the abyss through destroyed parts of the road.
Genre: Adventure/Arcade
Platform: PC│Web
Tools: Unity3D, Blender, Adobe Photoshop
Project Length: 72 hours

Work done/Challenges

  • All the programming and level design in Unity3D.
  • Corruption and cleansing effect of buildings and road tiles. Buildings and tiles are stored in lists and chosen randomly from it to be corrupted.
  • Minimap system using Camera and Render Texture.
